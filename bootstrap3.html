The Bangladesh National Bird


The magpie robin (Copsychus saularis) is the national bird of Bangladesh, where it is common and known as the Doyel. They are common birds in urban gardens as well as forests.

They are particularly well known for their songs and were once popular as cagebirds. It is a widely used symbol in Bangladesh, appearing on currency notes, and a landmark in the city of Dhaka is named the Doyel Chatwar.
